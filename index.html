On a clear day, the famous pyramids of Egypt are visible from downtown Cairo. The morning I visited the Pyramids, the weather was not clear; the air was thick with smog and haze. The streets were calm and it was an easy taxi ride from the hotel to the Pyramids. As I drew closer, the pyramids slowly revealed themselves in the haze. As I stood at the base of the pyramids, the hazy sky added to the overall majestic and mystical feeling of the experience. The haze gradually dissipated, the sky cleared, and I was afforded a clear, magnificent view of one of the seven wonders of the ancient world.
